LIGHTBOX

    WRITTEN TUTORIAL

  • Click on an image to select it. Click and hold to drag it.
  • Rotate or resize a selected image using the squares on the border around it.
  • Use the buttons on the banner to crop an image, remove an image, or save/load a Lightbox session.
  • Go back to the William Blake Archive tab or window to add more images to the Lightbox.
