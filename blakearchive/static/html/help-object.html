OBJECT VIEW

Click the title in the banner to change copies.

Click the icons to the right or left of the object to go to the next or previous object, respectively, in the group.

Click the object to open it in a new window or tab, depending on how your browser is set.

Browse the critical apparatus via the 'i' window and the tabs below.

Click 'G' in the banner to enter Gallery Mode. In Gallery Mode, objects will open by default in the Object View.

Click 'R' in the banner to enter Reading Mode. In Reading Mode, objects will open by default in the Reading View.
