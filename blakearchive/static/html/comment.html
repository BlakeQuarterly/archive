The William Blake Archive welcomes any comments or suggestions you may have.

If you are writing with a specific question or request, we encourage you to consult our list of Frequently Asked Questions.

If you are writing to request permission to reproduce an image or a transcription, please email us: williamblakearchive@gmail.com. We can only grant permission to reproduce Archive materials; please do not contact us regarding materials not present in the Archive.

Finally, please be certain to sign your comments with a valid return email address, included in the body—and not just the header—of your message; if you do not receive a reply to a question or comment, it's because we've been unable to contact you.
